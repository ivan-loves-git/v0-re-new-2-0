## 3. DECISION LOG & STRATEGIC PIVOTS

### Location: `/03_DECISION_LOG/`

**Critical Decisions (November 2024 - Dual Pivot Month):**

#### Decision 1: DEC-20241101-dual-monetization-testing.md
**Title:** "Test Both Monetization Streams Simultaneously"
- **Date:** Early November 2024
- **Decision:** Test partner-side (commission) + repreneur-side (subscription) monetization in parallel for 3-6 months
- **Why:** Insufficient data to choose between models; parallel testing accelerates learning
- **Experiment Duration:** November 2024 - May 2025
- **Success Criteria:**
  - Partner-side: ‚â•2/10 partners agree, ‚â•1 deal closes, ‚â•‚Ç¨25K revenue
  - Repreneur-side: ‚â•3/10 repreneurs pay, ‚â•‚Ç¨1K/month revenue, <50% churn

#### Decision 2: DEC-20241101-ats-to-crm-operational-pivot.md
**Title:** "ATS is Dead, CRM is the Future"
- **Date:** Early November 2024
- **Decision:** Shift from Applicant Tracking System (recruitment model) to Customer Relationship Management (service delivery model)
- **Rationale:** Campaign #2 proved Re-New is not in recruitment business; repreneurs are customers and products simultaneously
- **Tool:** Migrating from Airtable ATS structure to Airtable CRM module
- **Enablers:** Tracking service delivery, monetization status, partner relationships
- **Impact:** Enables managing 50-100+ repreneurs at scale (current: 35 active + 35 pipeline)
