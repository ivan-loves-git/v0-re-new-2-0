## Platform Overview (Quick Recap)

**Re-New Wave 1.0** is a progressive digital platform serving acquisition entrepreneurs (repreneurs) through the SME acquisition journey in Europe.

### The Six-Tier Monetization Model

1. **Discovery (FREE)** - Readiness assessment, educational content, community read-only, 5 deal teasers/month
2. **Professional (€99/month)** - Analysis tools, Lead de Cadrage builder, partner directory, 20 deal views/month, full community
3. **Advanced (€249/month)** - Unlimited deals, advanced tools, bi-weekly office hours, negotiated partner discounts
4. **Premium (€349/month)** - Monthly 1-on-1 coaching, expert network access, deal support services
5. **Starter Pack (€1,250 one-time)** - Bertrand's 6-week intensive program + 3 months Advanced tier
6. **Operating Partner (€1,500-3,000/month)** - Post-acquisition unlimited strategic support

### Core Platform Capabilities

- Comprehensive readiness assessment (20 questions, 3 dimensions)
- Lead de Cadrage (acquisition criteria) builder
- Curated SME deal flow (France, expanding Europe)
- Financial analysis toolkit (valuation, DD checklists, models)
- Repreneur community platform (forums, mentorship, events)
- Vetted partner network (lawyers, accountants, lenders, brokers)
- Deal pipeline CRM and collaboration tools
- Expert coaching and advisory services (Bertrand + network)
- Post-acquisition support tools and frameworks

## The Challenge

The same platform can be positioned in multiple ways depending on:
- **Target audience** segment emphasis
- **Primary pain point** addressed
- **Competitive** differentiation angle
- **Emotional** appeal strategy
- **Market** maturity and readiness

This document explores **15 strategically viable value propositions** for Re-New Wave 1.0, analyzing how each positions the platform differently while delivering the same underlying features.

- Valuation calculator suite (DCF, comparables, asset-based)
