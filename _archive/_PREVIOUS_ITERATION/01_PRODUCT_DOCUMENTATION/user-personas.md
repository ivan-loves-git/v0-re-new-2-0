## Primary Personas

### Persona 1: The Explorer (Early Stage)

**"I want to own a business, but I don't know where to start."**

#### Demographics
- **Age:** 30-40 years old
- **Location:** France (primary), Italy (expansion)
- **Current Role:** Mid-level manager, consultant, or corporate executive
- **Experience:** 5-10 years professional experience
- **Education:** Bachelor's or Master's degree (business, engineering, or equivalent)
- **Income:** ‚Ç¨50-80K/year
- **Savings:** ‚Ç¨50-150K available for investment

#### Professional Background
- **Industries:** Consulting, tech, finance, operations
- **Skills:** Strategic thinking, analysis, project management
- **Limitations:** No P&L ownership, limited operational experience
- **Ambition:** Seeking ownership path vs. corporate ladder

#### Goals & Motivations
**Primary Goal:** Understand if SME acquisition is right for them

**Motivations:**
- üéØ **Autonomy:** Control their destiny, not dependent on corporate politics

- üöÄ **Impact:** Run something meaningful, preserve legacies

**Specific Objectives:**
- Assess personal readiness for acquisition entrepreneurship
- Understand the acquisition process and timeline
- Identify knowledge/skill gaps to address

## Anti-Personas

**Who We Do NOT Serve:**

### 1. The Tire-Kicker
- **Profile:** Curious but not committed, no capital, no timeline
- **Why Not:** Low conversion probability, drains advisory resources
- **Alternative:** Free content marketing, wait until serious

### 2. The Distressed Buyer
- **Profile:** Desperate for income, no savings, unrealistic expectations
- **Why Not:** High failure risk, mismatch with SME acquisition economics
- **Alternative:** Refer to employment services or franchising

### 3. The Empire Builder (Too Large)
- **Profile:** Seeking $50M+ acquisitions, institutional backing, private equity
- **Why Not:** Different market segment, different support needs
- **Alternative:** Refer to search fund accelerators or PE firms

### 4. The Micro-Acquirer (Too Small)
- **Profile:** Seeking <‚Ç¨500K businesses, solo operator, lifestyle focus
- **Why Not:** Economics don't support subscription model
- **Alternative:** Refer to business brokers or marketplaces

## Usage Insights & Design Implications

### Feature Priorities by Persona

**For Explorers (Discovery):**
1. Readiness Assessment - Self-diagnostic confidence builder
2. Lead de Cadrage - Structured criteria creation
3. Learning Paths - Education roadmap
4. Community Access - Peer validation
5. Consultation Booking - Expert validation

**For Active Searchers (Acquisition):**
1. Deal Flow Browse - Quality matching
2. Pipeline Tracker - Manage multiple opportunities
3. Due Diligence Tools - Execution support
4. Expert Network - On-demand specialists
5. Coaching - Real-time guidance

**For First-Time Owners (Post-Acquisition):**
1. Operating Partner Program - Unlimited advisory
2. Milestone Tracker - Structured transition
3. KPI Dashboard - Business health monitoring
4. Peer Advisory Circles - Community support
5. Expert Network - Specialized help

### Engagement Patterns

**Explorer:**
- **Login Frequency:** 2-3x/week
- **Session Length:** 15-30 minutes
- **Primary Actions:** Read, assess, explore
- **Conversion Signals:** Completes readiness quiz + Lead de Cadrage

**Active Searcher:**
- **Login Frequency:** Daily
- **Session Length:** 30-60 minutes
- **Primary Actions:** Browse deals, update pipeline, message experts
- **Conversion Signals:** Saved deals + coaching sessions + financing research

**First-Time Owner:**
- **Login Frequency:** Daily ‚Üí Weekly ‚Üí Monthly (as business stabilizes)
- **Session Length:** 10-45 minutes (varies by need)
- **Primary Actions:** Check KPIs, book coaching, ask questions
- **Conversion Signals:** Consistent KPI updates + coaching usage + peer engagement
