## ğŸ¯ Product Vision

**Mission:** Transform the European SME succession landscape by empowering acquisition entrepreneurs with systematic preparation, curated deal flow, and ongoing support throughout their entire journey.

**Product:** A subscription-based SaaS platform serving repreneurs from initial discovery through successful exit, replacing the traditional "candidates as products" model with "candidates as customers."

**Market:** European acquisition entrepreneurs (France primary, Italy expansion) seeking to acquire SME businesses in the â‚¬1-5M range.
