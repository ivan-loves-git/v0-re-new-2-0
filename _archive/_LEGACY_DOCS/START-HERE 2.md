## ‚ö†Ô∏è CRITICAL RULE: How to Change CANONICAL Files

### Files in `00_CANONICAL/` (Strategy, Brand, Operations, Knowledge)

**NEVER delete old content.** Instead:

1. Add new/updated content at the TOP of the relevant section
2. Move the old content to the BOTTOM of the file under a `## Change History` section
3. Strike through the old content using `~~strikethrough~~`
4. Add a timestamp and reason for the change

**Example:**

```markdown
## Business Model (Updated)

Our revenue model focuses on success-based fees from completed acquisitions...

---

## Change History

### Business Model (Previous - 2025-10-15)
~~We planned to charge candidates a flat tuition fee of ‚Ç¨5,000...~~

**Changed:** 2025-10-24
**Reason:** Market validation showed candidates prefer success-based model; reduces barrier to entry
```

**Why this matters:**
- Preserves evolution of strategic thinking
- Shows what we learned and when
- Enables "why did we change course?" questions
- Maintains accountability and decision context
